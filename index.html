FAQ

I'M VEGAN. IS THE BOSTON CREAM VEGAN?
Yes! Everything in the store is vegan! Even the boston cream!

I'M ALLERGIC TO NUTS! DO YOU USE NUTS?
Our flavors change daily, but we often use nuts as toppings. While the actual dough does not contain nuts, there is no guarantee they haven't come in contact while in the kitchen. We do our best, but when we're pumping out 1000+ donuts a day it's hard to keep them fully separate!

ALLERGIES — TELL ME MORE!
In addition to regularly using nuts in the kitchen, all of our donuts contain soy & wheat. We do not use dairy or eggs!
